ARE WE KEEPING UP WITH THE DEMANDS OF RECORD-KEEPING FOR FORENSIC ODONTOLOGY? TRENDS AND PRACTICES OF RECORD MAINTENANCE AMONG GENERAL DENTAL PRACTITIONERS OF GUJARAT

About Me

Throughout my dental education, I have consistently demonstrated a dedication to academic excellence, a deep understanding of dental principles, and a genuine enthusiasm for the intricate world of oral healthcare. Aim of the study

To evaluate the existing state of record keeping among the general dental practitioners of Gujarat to determine the knowledge and attitude towards forensic odontology in the state of Gujarat and assess the preparedness of general dental practitioners when the necessity for forensic purposes arises.

Guided By: Dr Rashmi Gubbi (HOD of the Department of Oral and Maxillofacial Pathology and Microbiology) (Manubhai Patel Dental College).
